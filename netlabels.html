Version 2.0 (13 July 2015) Netlabels are for sharing music, everyone must have the ability to share the music. Because of that netlabels generally do the following:
  • give their digital music away for free
  • use copy left licensing
  • do not release only one person
If your netlabel meets the above criteria or you need your entry updated, contact me via email (david.nemeth@gmail.com) or contact me on Twitter, @dpnem.
